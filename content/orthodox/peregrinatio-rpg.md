## What it is

Peregrinatio is a content project, not code. The repository is a codex — handouts, character sheets, quick references, and narrator tools styled as a 9th-century manuscript and written for direct use at the table. Files can be printed, shared, or adapted.

## Contents

- **Campaign chronicle** — a living hagiography where missions, relics, scars, and blessings are recorded
- **Character sheets** — one per archetype (Knight, Navigator, Monastic, Illuminator), with Virtues, Abilities, Companions, Wounds, and Inventory
- **Inventory sheet** — a shared store of materials and relics for the fellowship
- **Mission log** (player-facing) and **mission tracker** (narrator-facing) — one side records, the other frames
- **Narrator quick reference** — difficulty, scene flow, conversion, supernatural foes, consequence/reward tables
- **Saints quick-roll** — a d20 intercession table for invoking holy aid

Included with each sheet: short prayers, the kind of thing you speak aloud at the table once per mission.
